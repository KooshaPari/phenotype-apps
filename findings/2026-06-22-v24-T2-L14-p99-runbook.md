## 1. What p99 means
p99 is the 99th-percentile latency: 99/100 requests complete in ≤ this time.
The slowest 1% is ignored, so p99 is the tail-latency signal SRE cares about
(Google SRE Book ch. 4). Computed from the Prometheus histogram
`http_request_duration_seconds_bucket` via
`histogram_quantile(0.99, sum by (le, …) (rate(...[5m])))`. **SLO target for
the fleet: p99 ≤ 50 ms** (panel 4 draws the line at 0.050 s).
- Panel 1: per-crate p99 timeseries — find the offending crate first.
- Panel 2: heatmap — spot distribution skew (one big bucket = hot path).
- Panel 3: fleet-wide p95 vs p99 — large gap = fat tail, investigate.
- Panel 4: same as panel 3 with the 50 ms SLO line drawn.
- Panel 5: 1h burn-rate gauge (companion to v24 T5 multi-window alerts).
- Panel 6: top-5 slowest endpoints — start triage here.

## 2. Alert thresholds
| Metric | Threshold | Window | Severity | Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fleet p99 | > 50 ms | 5 min, sustained 10 min | warn | check panel 1 / 6 |
| Fleet p99 | > 100 ms | 5 min, sustained 5 min | page | page on-call |
| Burn rate (1h) | > 1.0 | 1 hour | warn | review panel 5 |
| Burn rate (1h) | > 2.0 | 1 hour | page | page on-call |
| Burn rate (6h) | > 1.0 | 6 hour | warn | post-mortem candidate |
| Per-crate p99 | > 2× crate budget | 15 min | warn | check `perf-budgets.toml` |
Multi-window alerts live in `alerts/slo-burn-rate.yaml` (v24 T5) per ADR-091.
Budget = 99.9% availability over 30 days = 43.2 min total error budget.

## 3. On-call response
1. **Acknowledge page in PagerDuty within 5 min.** Note the dashboard URL.
2. **Identify the offender:** read panel 6 (top-5 slowest) → panel 1 (per-crate).
3. **Snapshot the dashboard** (camera icon → PNG) for the post-mortem.
4. **If p99 > 100 ms sustained 5 min:** open incident channel `#inc-p99-<date>`.
5. **Mitigate first, fix root cause second:** rollback if the regression is
   tied to a recent deploy (`git revert` → push → wait 5 min for re-baseline).
6. **If sustained > 30 min OR error-budget burn rate > 2×:** freeze deploys
   (post in `#deploys`). Page secondary on-call.
7. **Within 24h:** file `findings/<date>-p99-incident.md` with timeline, root
   cause, contributing factors, action items (per `incident-postmortem` skill).
8. **Within 1 week:** verify the fix ships, the panel stays green for 7
   consecutive days, and the burn rate returns to < 0.5.
