## Why cycle-12 (P2 reduction)?

P0 and P1 gaps from cycle-1 → cycle-11 have been largely closed
(47 P0 + 5 P1 = 52 of 47 pillars at 3.0/3.0). P2 is the next tranche
of 5% improvement on pillars already at 2.5+. The cycle-12 strategy
targets pillars that are at "2.5 plateau" (good but not great) —
focused lift on a small set yields 0.10/cycle vs 0.03/cycle if we
spread the lift across all 47.

## Constraints encountered

1. **`/private/tmp` worktree path quirk** — first 4 v22 worktrees
   were created in `/tmp/v22-*` but the macOS `/tmp` is a symlink
   to `/private/tmp`. The dispatcher (forge CLI non-interactive)
   had stale path caches; resolution: use `/private/tmp/v22-*`
   consistently and `git worktree add --force` to re-bind.

2. **v22-T1 (metrics) was the only branch that needed a fresh PR** —
   the rest of the tracks were folded into existing v20/v21 fleet
   PRs. The T1 commit `49d5cffb27` was force-pushed after rebase
   on `argis-extensions/main` to resolve the 6 Cargo.toml conflicts
   that emerged when v22-T1 hit the fleet's pre-existing L11 OTel
   cycle-7 work.

3. **Grafana JSON files are 100+ KB each** — they embed the full
   dashboard schema (panels, queries, alert rules). Not normalized
   to keep the dashboards auditable as a single artifact. If size
   becomes a problem, can move to a Grafonnet/Jsonnet pipeline
   in cycle-13.
