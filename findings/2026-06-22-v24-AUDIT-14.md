# AUDIT-14 — Sparse-checkout cone vs declared members (governance)

**Date:** 2026-06-22
**Domain:** Governance (monorepo hygiene, ADR-028)
**Severity:** P2 (informational; affects C4 diagram accuracy)
**Method:** `cat .git/info/sparse-checkout` + `ls -d pheno-* phenotype-*` + count.

## Finding

Sparse-checkout cone was not directly readable in this audit (file not in cone), but observable consequence: 15 `pheno-*` directories visible, 47+ `pheno-*` crates claimed per v17 boundary doc, 18+ `phenotype-registry-*` clones visible. The discrepancy (15 vs 47) is documented in AUDIT-01.

Other cone-vs-claim discrepancies:

- `pheno-config` exists in cone; v17 doc claims `pheno-config` is the canonical substrate. ✓
- `pheno-errors` exists in cone at `pheno-errors/Cargo.toml:2` version 0.1.0. ✓
- `pheno-tracing` at `0.3.0-pre.0` is prerelease. ✓ but version skew with siblings (see AUDIT-02).
- `pheno-secret-scan` exists in cone as directory. ✓
- `pheno-scaffold-kit`, `pheno-ssot-template`, `pheno-llms-txt`, `pheno-vibecoding-guard`, `pheno-worklog-schema`, `pheno-drift-detector`, `pheno-framework-lint`, `pheno-predict` — directories exist but Cargo.toml NOT in cone (likely Python or no Cargo at all).

## Impact

The sparse-checkout cone is **not declared in a manifest file** readable from this branch. The C4 component diagram (T1) will need to either (a) expand the cone or (b) accept a partial inventory.

## Recommendation

- Add a `meta/INVENTORY.md` at fleet root enumerating every pheno-* / phenotype-* / federated service with: present-in-cone (Y/N), latest-version, primary-language.
- The pheno-drift-detector (ADR-049) should run on every `Cargo.toml` change and emit a cone-vs-claim diff to CI logs.

**Files cited:** `pheno-errors/Cargo.toml:2`; `pheno-tracing/Cargo.toml:2`; 15 pheno-* directory names; 18 phenotype-registry-* clone names.
