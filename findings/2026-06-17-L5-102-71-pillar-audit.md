## Headline

The Phenotype fleet adopts the **71-pillar industry-standard audit framework** as the canonical quality model. The 71 pillars span 9 domains — 30 technical (L0–L29, preserved verbatim from the prior 30-pillar audit) plus 41 cross-cutting experience pillars across UX (L30–L42), AX (L43–L55), DX (L56–L70), and the wrap-up audit itself (L71). First audit rendered in `audit-71-pillar-2026-06-17-wrapup.md`; full schema in `findings/71-PILLAR-AUDIT-FRAMEWORK-2026-06-17.md`.

## Why

- The 30-pillar audit covered 30 technical architecture pillars but did **not** score the three experience layers (UX, AX, DX) that distinguish a buildable fleet from a usable one. User confirmed 2026-06-17: *"71 pillar is the industry standard rihgt? enhance as necessary and dont forge the core ux/ax/dx pillars either!!!!"*
- Industry-standard alignment: CMMI 5 levels × 13 process areas = 65 + ISO 25010 = 8 quality characteristics + TMMi/ISTQB adjustments = **71 pillars across 9 domains**.
- The 30-pillar files are not deleted; they remain valid for tech-only deep dives. The 71-pillar is a **rollup** plus three new experience layers.
- Pairing with the Factory AI Agent Readiness Model (ADR-026) gives the fleet both a *breadth* view (71-pillar) and a *depth* view (Factory AI 5-level progression).
