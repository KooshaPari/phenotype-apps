## Pattern reaffirmed

Heredoc + Python is the only reliable mutation pattern. The 85-file commit
was assembled by writing each of 15 × 12 = 180 files via heredoc in a
single shell invocation, then staging them all at once. Any attempt to
use `patch` / `write` / `sed -i` individually caused mid-session reverts
due to the shared-lock-DAG (26+ parallel subagents).
