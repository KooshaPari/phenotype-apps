### Track T2 — L52 Encryption-at-Rest Mandate  ✅ **DONE** (L5-154, 2026-06-21)

**Owner:** orchestrator (delegated to forge L5-154 subagent for ADR authorship)
**Wall-clock:** ~3 days
**LOC:** ~300 (mostly docs + 1 CI check)
**Status:** **DONE 2026-06-21** — ADR landed at **`docs/adr/2026-06-21/ADR-080-encryption-at-rest-mandate.md`** (numbering deviation from plan: ADR-078 → **ADR-080** per task spec; the Vault migration roadmap occupies ADR-079 by task spec).

Per v18 probe §6 finding 2, **L52 (Encryption-at-Rest)** sat at 1.5 because no fleet-wide policy existed. Substrate crates (pheno-config, pheno-port-adapter) handled secrets in plaintext in memory, with no `zeroize` enforcement.

**Deliverables (updated per task scope):**

1. `docs/adr/2026-06-21/ADR-080-encryption-at-rest-mandate.md` (371 LOC, ~5 pages) — **LANDED**
   - Policy: encryption-at-rest mandated for **4 fleet-critical substrates**: `pheno-otel` (NDJSON traces), `pheno-events` (append-only journal), `pheno-predict` (LMDB + Pickle state caches), `phenotype-registry` (SQLite via SQLCipher).
   - Cipher selection: AES-256-GCM default; ChaCha20-Poly1305 software fallback; AES-256-XTS volume-layer only.
   - Authoritative sources: NIST SP 800-57 Part 1 Rev. 5 (§ 5.3.1, § 5.6, § 6.2.1.3 — Argon2id mandate), NIST SP 800-38D (§ 5.2.1.1, § 8.2 — 96-bit random IV), CIS Benchmark v8 (AWS § 2.1.1, § 2.1.5; Linux § 1.4.1; Kubernetes § 5.5.1; macOS § 1.2).
   - 3-phase rollout: (1) Discovery + transitional Argon2id keys, (2) Vault transit integration (gated on ADR-079 Phase 1 GA), (3) Audit + FedRAMP SC-28 / SOC2 CC6.1 evidence.
   - Devcontainer + Dockerfile: encrypted tmpfs + `cap_add: [IPC_LOCK]` for `mlock`.
   - AWS: EBS + S3 default encryption enforced (terraform reference in ADR).
   - Kubernetes: StorageClass with Vault-injected secret + NetworkPolicy.

2. `.cargo/audit-rules.toml` (50 LOC) — *deferred to follow-up PR; not part of ADR scope*
3. `pheno-config/src/secrets.rs` `#[derive(ZeroizeOnDrop)]` — *deferred to follow-up PR; not part of ADR scope*

**Score target:** L52 1.5 → 2.0 (re-audit 2026-06-28 cycle-9 refresh per ADR-041)
